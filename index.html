Learn About Us

A. B. Sulu-Gambari & Co. is a modern and dynamic firm of legal practitioners based in Lagos. The firm was founded in 1997 and has expanded quite considerably over the years. It enjoys the wealth of experience of its partners who have each put in not less than eighteen (18) years at the Bar.

With the privilege of enjoying the consultancy and cooperation of reputable law firms abroad and possessing at its apex, the services of a retired Justice of the Court of Appeal as Consultant, we offer sound legal services based on our international and local expertise.

Our Practices Areas

Civil Law

Civil law is a branch of the legal system that deals with disputes and conflicts between individuals, organizations, or entities, typically involving issues such as contracts, property rights, family law, and personal injury.

Family Law

Family law is a branch of legal practice that deals with matters related to family relationships and domestic issues. It encompasses a wide range of legal issues and concerns that affect individuals and families.

Learn More

Business Law

Business law, also known as commercial law or corporate law, is a branch of legal practice that deals with legal issues and regulations related to business activities and commercial transactions.

Learn More

Education Law

The legal system that deals with issues related to education, schools, and educational institutions. It encompasses a wide range of legal matters that impact students, teachers, parents, and educational administrators.

Criminal Law

Criminal law is a branch of legal practice that deals with the prosecution of individuals or entities accused of committing criminal offenses.
