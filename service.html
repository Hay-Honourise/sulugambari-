Practice Areas

Our Practices Areas

Admiralty and Shipping

A.B. Sulu-Gambari & Co., has a vibrant admiralty and shipping practice group, which is at the forefront of admiralty and shipping practice in Nigeria. Our Clients include Ship Owners, Protection and Indemnity (P & I) Clubs, P & I Correspondents, Receivers of Cargo, Freight Forwarders and Charterers all over the world. The firm has been largely involved in the formulation of shipping and management policies and was the lead shipping adviser in a core Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) project. We are members of the Nigerian Maritime Law Association.

Energy and Natural Resources

Our Energy and Natural Resources Practice Group consists of knowledgeable and experienced practitioners well abreast of the modern trends and developments in the Energy and Natural Resources space. We provide regulatory compliance, advisory and due diligence support to individuals and corporate players within the energy and natural resources industry, in accordance with legal and the industry requirements. We are currently on the legal advisory team of a gas pipeline project that is perhaps the biggest in Nigeria today.

⁠Taxation

At A.B. Sulu – Gambari & Co., our underlying philosophy is to fully utilize all exemptions and incentives provided by the tax laws and regulations for the benefit of our Clients We have a strong team of specialists handling all tax-related matters including disputes which are bound to arise between tax authorities, domestic/international corporations and financial institutions trading in Nigeria.

⁠Capital Markets

The Nigerian capital market is a regulated market for long term investments where corporate equities and long-term debt securities are issued and traded. We are registered with the Nigerian Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as Capital Market Operators. Our capital market practice group comprises of lawyers with in depth understanding of the international and domestic capital markets transactions who have an excellent grasp of the dynamics of the market and its regulations. We are able to provide expert advice to our Clients in connection with all types of offerings including public offerings and private placements of debt and equity.

⁠Litigation and Alternative Dispute Resolution

Our litigation and dispute resolution practice group consists of litigators and certified arbitrators representing Clients locally and internationally. We are well equipped to help you navigate commercial disputes in and out of the courts, while securing your best interest. We have represented Clients in various superior courts and arbitral tribunals in Nigeria and abroad. We represented a Client in a London maritime arbitration where we won the sum of USD700,000.00 for our Client. We were also successful on appeal to the High Court of England.

Banking and Finance

The firm’s Banking and Finance practice group is recognized for its domestic and cross border financial transactions. In recent times, we have advised borrowers and lenders on debt, asset acquisition and project finance. We have a team of lawyers equipped to handle banking and finance related matters.

About Us

A. B. Sulu-Gambari & Co. is a modern and dynamic firm of legal practitioners based in Lagos. The firm was founded in 1997 and has expanded quite considerably over the years.
